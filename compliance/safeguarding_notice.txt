Safeguarding Notice
===================

If you believe someone is being abused or neglected in a care setting,
please contact:

1. In an emergency, call 999

2. Contact your local authority safeguarding team:
   https://www.gov.uk/report-abuse-of-older-person

3. Report concerns to CQC:
   - Online: https://www.cqc.org.uk/give-feedback-on-care
   - Phone: 03000 616161

4. Contact the Safeguarding Adults helpline:
   - Ann Craft Trust: 0115 951 5400

CareGist is a directory service. We do not provide care, inspect
providers, or handle safeguarding concerns. If you have urgent concerns
about someone's safety, contact the authorities listed above immediately.
