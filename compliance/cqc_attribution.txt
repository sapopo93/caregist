CQC Data Attribution Notice
===========================

The care provider information displayed on CareGist is sourced from the
Care Quality Commission (CQC), the independent regulator of health and
social care in England.

CareGist is NOT an official CQC service. We present CQC data in a more
searchable and comparable format to help families and professionals find
care providers.

For official inspection reports and the most up-to-date information,
always visit: https://www.cqc.org.uk

Data freshness: CareGist refreshes its data regularly, but there may be
a delay between CQC publishing updated information and it appearing here.
Always check the "last updated" date on each provider listing.

If you have concerns about the quality of care at any provider, contact
CQC directly:
  - Website: https://www.cqc.org.uk/contact-us
  - Phone: 03000 616161
