03 — Brand Concept

The Gist of Good Care

CareGist means getting to the heart of it — the essential truth of a care provider, stripped of jargon. Like the Claude brand's warmth and intelligence, CareGist feels human and approachable rather than clinical or corporate.

Human-first Trustworthy Grounded

Earth as a Signal of Trust

Terracotta, bark, moss and parchment are the colours of nature, age, and permanence — evoking warmth, stability, and genuine care. They stand in sharp contrast to the clinical blues and cold whites dominating health tech.

Warm Credible Distinct

Logomark — The Cg Tile

A rounded square tile in Clay with the "Cg" monogram in Playfair Display, anchored by an Amber accent dot — evoking a wax seal or stamp of quality. Simple enough to work as a favicon, distinctive enough to own.

Scalable Memorable Authority

Voice & Tone

Warm but direct. Expert but never cold. CareGist speaks to worried families and busy managers alike — with the honest, measured tone of a trusted professional, not a faceless platform. Think: knowledgeable friend.

Clear Empathetic Expert

06 — Why This Works

A brand that feels like a trusted hand, not a cold platform.

Claude.ai's warmth comes from its earthy coral and parchment tones — CareGist pushes that further into deeper terracottas, forest moss, and aged bark, grounding the brand in nature rather than tech.

The serif pairing (Playfair Display + Lora) signals expertise and permanence — the kind of authority families trust when choosing care for a loved one. DM Sans keeps the UI clean and readable without losing that warmth.

Against a sector dominated by NHS blues, cold whites, and generic sans-serifs, CareGist will stand out immediately. Earth tones read as honest, human, and safe — exactly the associations you need in a care directory.
